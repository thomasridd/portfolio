LaSwap Self Entry

A full HTML5 web app that was designed to smooth the admission of new students.

Full data on student GCSE performance is an invaluable resource for teachers during their first lessons but has never been an option entered manually. This year I built an app for students to enter the grades themselves.

The app worked. The teachers get their grades. My team gets gold dust in terms of some other difficult to acquire data. The continuity planning implications are not good but otherwise this was an unqualified success.
