iOS Apps

Skills Used: iOS - Objective-C - Geocoding - Google Maps for iOS - CoreData - In-App Purchase - SPARQL - The Semantic Web - JSON

I have developed two iOS apps that have been released on the AppStore - Crowflies Map Manager for iPad and WikiWhere for iPhone. Neither are going to outsell Angry Birds but both are polished apps with a professional look and feel.

WikiWhere plots Wikipedia articles with geolocation markers nearby a central point. It draws data from several sources including the Semantic Web using the NoSQL technology SPARQL. Crowflies is a distance measurement app expanded to an advanced functionality. Tech used includes CoreData, In-App purchases, Google Maps for iOS and the Google Directions API.
