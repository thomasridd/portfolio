Hackathons - Angelhack

Skills Used: iPad - iPhone - APIs - Python - Twilio - MongoDB - Geocoding - Google App Engine - Team Development

I have adopted hackathons as a good way to get in touch with the general dev community, learn fresh skills, and gain involvement working in a team development situation.

My team mates were all full time devs and I learnt a lot from working with them. This includes the confidence that my software development level is equal to or well above many currently working in industry so where my team mates brought android and web development experience I could bring geocoding, iOS, and a willingness to tackle fresh tech on the spot.
