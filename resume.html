Our History

I am a data manager and analyst looking to apply a strong quantitative research background, excellent analytic ability, and proven programming skills to the field of data science.

I graduated from The University of Warwick with 2:1 BSc Honours in Applied Mathematics in 2000. In 2001 I graduated from Warwick Business School with an MSc in Management Science and Operational Research which earned a distinction and went on to do post- graduate research in this field.

I have a passion for software development and thrive in a highly motivated team environment. I have released two iOS apps on the AppStore and have competed in a number of hackathons. Here I have demonstrated value amidst teams of experienced developers; in the most recent event I was one of four members of the winning team.

I have taken the initiative to expand my skills into data science, both through online courses and practical application. I have researched and learnt R, Python and MongoDB, to name just three, developing these in practice by integrating them into my work and personal projects.
